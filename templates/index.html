About

Anemia is a condition that develops when your blood produces a lower-than-normal amount of healthy red blood cells...

MCH

Mean Corpuscular Hemoglobin (MCH) measures the average amount of hemoglobin in your red blood cells.

MCHC

Mean Corpuscular Hemoglobin Concentration (MCHC) measures the concentration of hemoglobin in your red blood cells.

MCV

Mean Corpuscular Volume (MCV) measures the average size of your red blood cells.
